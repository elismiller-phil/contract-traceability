# Contract Lifecycle Ontology Prototype

This mini-project models a defense services contract as a lifecycle and traceability problem.

The case centers on Contract `W912DY-23-C-0047`, awarded by Air Mobility Command to Talisman Logistics Solutions and later novated to Beacon Defense Industries after a corporate acquisition.

The ontology represents:

- contract identity and continuity
- contract modifications
- organizational parties
- legal contractor substitution through novation
- contracting officer and COR roles
- performance periods and option exercise
- funding changes
- deliverable changes
- stop-work and resume-work events
- administrative closeout

## Main Modeling Insight

The contract persists as a legal and administrative artifact despite changes in scope, funding, personnel roles, performance state, and contractor identity.
